When Can You Get HarvardKey?

HarvardKey is the University's new unified credential for access to a wealth of online resources spanning the Harvard Community. Users across Harvard will be eligible to claim a HarvardKey this fall/winter for simpler, more secure access to the applications and services you use every day. If your School or unit isn't in the eligibility list below, simply choose the appropriate tab at the top of this login screen for your usual login type.

Harvard Alumni are already eligible to claim a HarvardKey, and users in the following units may claim a HarvardKey beginning November 12:

  • Faculty of Arts & Sciences
  • Central Administration
  • Graduate School of Education
  • Graduate School of Design
  • Harvard Divinity School
  • Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health
  • Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences
